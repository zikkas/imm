## Course Syllabus

### Part I: Analytic Number Theory (Prof. Salah Eddine Rihane)

- **Arithmetic Functions:** Dirichlet convolution, Mobius inversion, Euler's Multipliers.
- **Distribution of Primes:** Chebyshev's estimates and the Prime Number Theorem.
- **L-Functions:** The Riemann Zeta function $\zeta(s)$, Dirichlet characters, and Dirichlet L-functions.
- **Analytic Continuation:** Functional equations and critical strips.

### Part II: Algebraic Number Theory

- **Number Fields:** Extensions of $\mathbb{Q}$, trace, norm, and discriminants.
- **Rings of Integers:** Integral bases, Dedekind domains, and factorization of ideals.
- **The Ideal Class Group:** Finiteness of the class number.
- **Unit Theory:** Dirichlet's Unit Theorem.
- **Valuation Theory:** $p$-adic numbers and local fields.
